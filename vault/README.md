# Research Wiki

This vault is my persistent research knowledge base.

It is not a dump of random notes.
It is a structured, interlinked wiki maintained with LLM assistance.

## Purpose

The goal of this wiki is to accumulate research knowledge over time instead of rediscovering it from raw documents in every new session.

Raw sources live in `10_sources/`.
The wiki layer distills, structures, cross-references, and updates knowledge across:
- source summaries
- concepts
- methods
- datasets
- people and institutions
- projects
- syntheses

## Core principle

Raw sources are the source of truth.
The wiki is the maintained interpretation layer.
Claude writes and updates the wiki.
I curate sources, direct the analysis, and review important changes.

## Main directories

- `00_inbox/` — unsorted incoming notes and materials
- `10_sources/` — raw, immutable source materials
- `20_summaries/` — one page per source
- `30_concepts/` — concept and mechanism pages
- `40_methods/` — methodology pages
- `50_datasets/` — dataset pages
- `60_people_institutions/` — author, institution, journal, and organization pages
- `70_projects/` — project and paper pages
- `90_synthesis/` — higher-level syntheses, comparisons, and research overviews
- `attachments/` — local images and attached files

## Key files

- `index.md` — content index of the wiki
- `log.md` — chronological record of ingests, queries, and maintenance
- `CLAUDE.md` — schema and workflow rules for maintaining this wiki

## Working philosophy

The wiki should become more useful over time.
New sources should update existing knowledge, not just create isolated notes.
Useful analyses should be written back into the wiki.
Contradictions, missing pages, and stale claims should be surfaced and resolved.
